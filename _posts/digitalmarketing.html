Digital marketing has taken the world in storm but there are some people wishing to enter this theme for the first time. They are quite new to the idea and it becomes important for them to know some terms before starting on the path of digital marketing. We will discuss the topics in details as

  1. Content marketing - Strategic marketing which involves creating and distributing contents which are valuable, resourceful, relevant to a particular topic which is intended for a particular targeted audience.
  2. Traffic analytics - Traffic analytics is a quick and efficient way to get the information of any website traffic channel, geographic distribution, visitor behavior, trends in action etc.
  3. SEO - SEO or Search Engine Optimization is the process of modifying or changing the website content or design so as to increase the visibility of the website in the search engines like google, bing, yahoo etc.
  4. Social media - Social media refers to websites, applications used by people to share contents quickly, reliably and in real time. Nowadays social media like twitter, Instagram, Facebook etc. are powerful tools for customer engagement in real time.
  5. Blogs - A blog or a weblog is a simple website used for discussion or sharing information on a particular topic related to politics, sports, marketing etc. Today it is very easy and effective to start a blog and share ideas and content with anyone with the help of world wide web. You can start your blog now from Wordpress
  6. PPC - PPC stands for Pay-per-click and it is a type of online marketing where in the advertisers pay a fee each time their ad is clicked. So it is basically one type of paid service for marketing.
  7. CPC - CPC stands for Cost-per-click and is the actual price the advertiser pays for the PPC model of marketing.
  8. Influencer marketing - Influencer marketing is a social media marketing where influencers endorse the products and services to a particular audience. An influencer is paid for endorsing a particular brand and this type marketing has been considered effective than other channels of marketing.
